Projects I have built so far:

SumoPi (2021->2022)

Competition organised by Department of cybernetics of the Faculty of Applied Sciences. This competition was about building the best sumo robot. I was planning to make it actually intelligent, so I started working on raspberry pi 4B with camera, 1 ToF sensor, Accelerometer and 2 line sensors. It was more like a State-of-art to prove the concept of using the Camera in a sumo robot, because i was the only one who was making sumo robot with camera in that competition. Conclusion: I managed to actually prove some efficiency with the camera, because the robot was able to efficiently detect white bounding lines of sumo field. Unfortunately, I did not have a time to tweak that code for better than State-of-art results.

Dronogeddon (2022->now)

This is the project from G+SOÅ  hackathon, because after the hackathon has finished, me and my team thought it would be nice to continue working on this interesting project. Basically, we are trying to code a drone which will fly in a house and predict human by camera, to find an intruder. This problematic was introduced to us in the hackathon, so we have only State-of-art results, but we are trying to find funding to buy programmable drone with more sensors to release better versions of Dronogeddon.
